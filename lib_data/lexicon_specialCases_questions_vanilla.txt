# Where was Obama born?
*	*	S[wq]/S[q]	(S[wq]{X}/S[q]{X}){_}	(lambda $f1 $X (exists $_ (and ($f1 $X) (EVENT $X $X at.2 $_ ENTITY) (QUESTION $_))))

# Hilton hotel -> hotel Hilton
